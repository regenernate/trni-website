The whole is meant to be simple and instructive.

So:

  1. Return to the terms list ( using the menu ).
  2. Click on the term you'd like to start with.
  3. Read the definition ( and other information ).
  4. Click on the next term that interests you
    ( there will eventually be several related terms to pick from )

That's it. The journey through these 50ish terms will be different, depending on which term you start with.

What did we miss? Please provide feedback by emailing nate @ theRegenerNativeInstitute . com.

And enjoy! We hope you leave with a better understanding of the potential for Regenerative Land Management.
