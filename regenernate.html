What is the Regenernation?

The regenernation is an intentional return to a true state of appreciation for our natural heritage. The commons. That which is ours to nurture and soothe us.

It begins with the literal ground you walk on. Your path to and from work, maybe. The trail out your back door that leads to the woods?

In the end it is simple. The regenernation is my home. It's our home. It's the United States, the world ... you know ... planet Earth.

Still kinda abstract ... what IS it?

Well, its a combination of several for-profit businesses with a not-for-profit, all focused on strengthening local food security through education and installation of RegenerNative habitats.

Ok, so who, or what is Regenernate?

Interesting question. We are still trying to figure that out ourselves.

Although regenernate started out as a clever email address for an individual, it quickly became representative of something a bit more grand.

As for the something grander, here is what we now believe to be true at Regenernate:

  • Humans have a far greater capacity for sensing, understanding, and engaging with the living world around us than we let ourselves believe.
  • Humans are capable of constantly doing better in all aspects of relationship with the living world, be it with other humans, with animals, with forests or even individual trees.
  • Humans can change. We can change in ways that are meaningful. In ways that persist. Ways that continually make the Earth a better place for all living things that call her home.

How do we Make It?

The RegenerNation, built by Regenernators is a practice of integrating landscapes with the living things that dwell upon them.

This includes encouraging the conditions for a comfortable re-integration of people into their proper place on the land they inhabit.

With a touch of patience, a lot of hard work, and the constant grounding to be found in daily work for this living world we call home, this work can be done.

This is the work of the Regenernation.
