Our Educational Offerings

The RegenerNative Institute is a for-profit multi-species ( and age ) program run by the same folks who are responsible for this web content.

It is in-person, outdoors, and sometimes also virtual, indoors. Individual groups can be small or large, depending upon the lesson(s) of the day.

Among other things, we color, draw, make music, dance, learn to identify living things, learn to walk quietly in the woods, and practice practical math.

Our policies ... on brief(s).

  1. Our educators and students are expected to behave in a non-denominational manner, and we do not tolerate discrimination for any reason.

  2. Otherwise, we look for and encourage safe, sweet, tolerant, helpful and kind actions and words where possible.

  3. And we require courteous language, even in midst of disagreement.

Violation of these policies may result in expulsion from the school and its premesis.

Into the hands of an aforeundersigned parent or guardian :).

Costs and class sizes

We offer flexible pricing to meet the needs, expectations and capacities of each potential "student/family unit."

For instance, if one were interested in a specific discipline or skill, we could set up a workshop with a per day rate ( like $20 per hour minimum 5 hours + materials and fuel ).

Alternatively, if one wanted to enroll a child for a full season of coursework, this could range from $150 to $1800 per month, depending on the number of classes and the resources needed internally to teach skills at an appropriate level.
